Mongolian Beef

Description

This Homemade Mongolian Beef is the perfect (under 30-minute) recipe the whole family will love. The crispy yet tender beef is amazing with the garlic and ginger sweet and savory sauce.

Ingredients

  • Mongolian Beef Ingredients

    • 1 carrot, peeled and julienned
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 red bell pepper
    • 1 Tbsp unsalted butter
    • 2 Tbsp oil
    • 1 lb flank steak
    • 1/4 cup cornstarch

  • Sauce Ingredients

    • 1 tsp fresh ginger
    • 4 garlic cloves
    • 1/3 cup brown sugar
    • 1/3 cup water
    • 1/3 cup low-sodium soy sauce
    • 1 tsp Sriracha, or added to taste
    • 1/3 cup green onions

Steps

  1. Place beef slices in a bowl, add cornstarch and stir to completely coat.
  2. In a bowl, combine the ingredients for the sauce and stir until well combined, set aside.
  3. Julienne the carrot and pepper, slice the onions. Thinly slice the beef into bite-sized strips.
  4. In a skillet, heat 1 Tbsp oil with 1 Tbsp butter over med/high heat. Add the carrot and peppers, saute to desired tenderness. Remove the vegetables from the skillet.
  5. Add 1 Tbsp oil over high heat. Once hot, add the beef and cook about 2 minutes per side.
  6. Add the vegetables back into the skillet with the green onion.
  7. Pour the sauce and stir to combine. Turn heat to medium/low and cook until the sauce thickens, about 3 minutes.
